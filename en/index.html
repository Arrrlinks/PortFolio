Berni

Berni is a robot made with other CESI students for the IBM competition, used to pick up cigarette butts on beaches in a given area, equipped with a roller in the middle to pick up the butts. The robot moves autonomously within the zone delimited by colored tape, which it detects thanks to its color sensor on the front of the robot. The robot is fitted with two batteries, a 24V one to power the motors and a 5V one to power the Arduino.
